How to Install the Film Length Calculator

iPhone (Safari)

  1. Open Safari (must be Safari, not Chrome).
  2. Go to: https://ncjdunn.github.io/Film-Calculator
  3. Tap the Share button (square with an arrow).
  4. Scroll and tap Add to Home Screen.
  5. Tap Add.

Android (Chrome, Edge, Brave)

  1. Open Chrome or another supported browser.
  2. Go to: https://ncjdunn.github.io/Film-Calculator
  3. Tap the 3-dot menu (top-right corner).
  4. Select Add to Home screen.
  5. Tap Add to confirm.
